State Specific Requirements

Alabama
Business Corporation (C-Corp) Suffix Requirements

Alabama requires the use of “Incorporated” or “Corporation.” Companies may also use an abbreviation of the same.

Prohibited Words

The state prohibits the use of words that imply impermissible purposes, such as “Olympic,” “Engineering,” “Engineer,” “Banc,” “Bank,” and “Banking.”

Alaska
Business Corporation (C-Corp) Suffix Requirements

Alaska requires the inclusion of “Company,” “Limited,” or “Incorporated.”

Prohibited Words

Words implying the business is a municipality, such as “City” and “Village,” are prohibited. Vulgar words or phrases are also prohibited.
